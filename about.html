About Me

I am a Data Analyst Professional and Computer Science graduate with strong expertise in machine learning, statistical analysis, and data visualization. I specialize in translating complex datasets into structured insights that drive strategic and operational decision-making.

During my tenure as a Data Science Intern at Ai Variant, I delivered end-to-end machine learning solutions including customer segmentation using K-Means clustering, a personalized recommendation engine utilizing collaborative filtering and Truncated SVD, and a solar power forecasting model built with Gradient Boosting Regression. My work involved data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, model evaluation, and deployment through interactive Streamlit dashboards.

I possess hands-on experience with Python, MySQL, NumPy, Pandas, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, along with practical knowledge of regression techniques, clustering algorithms, and time series forecasting. I am comfortable working across the full analytics lifecycle — from raw data extraction and cleaning to model development and business-facing visualization.

In addition to professional experience, I have developed a phishing website detection system using machine learning and fuzzy logic, demonstrating my ability to design scalable and security-oriented analytical solutions.

I am highly detail-oriented, analytical, and performance-driven, with a strong commitment to delivering measurable business value through data-driven solutions.
